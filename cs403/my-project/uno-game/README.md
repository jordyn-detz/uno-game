# Game Overview

UNO is a turn-based card game that is designed for 4 players. The goal of the game is to be the first player to get rid of all of their cards. The players will take turns playing cards from their hand that either matches the color or number of the current playing card from the playing pile or a special action card. If the player has no card that is playable they must draw a card from the draw pile and it switches to the next players turn. The different strategies come from deciding what type of card to play and when to employ your special actions  cards. The special action cards include, reverse, skip turn, or draw 2 which must match the color of the current playing card and a wild card which can be played at any time and allows the player to pick any color they want that card to be. 

# Equipment

- 4 players
- deck of cards (consisting of red, blue, yellow and green cards that have a number 1-4 or a specialty action: Reverse, Draw 2, Skip, Wild)
- Draw Pile (Unseen cards that players ca draw from when they don't have a playable card)
- Playing Pile (Stack where played cards are placed)

 # Setup

(1) Shuffle deck of cards.
(2) Deal 7 cards to every player.
(3) Flip the top card from the draw pile and place it in the playing pile. 
(4) Player 1 goes first and the game will proceed clockwise to begin.

# Turn Actions

During a players turn, they must complete one of the following options:

(1) Play a card from their hand that matches the current playing cards color or number (to include skip , draw 2, or reverse that match the color)
(2) Play a wild card ad select the new color for play
(3) Draw a card from the Draw pile if they have no playable cards. If the card drawn is playable they may immediatley play it, if not it moves to the next players turn
(4) If the player previous put down a draw 2 the current player must draw 2 cards from draw pile and their turn ends
(5) If the previous player put down a skip, the current player does not get to make any move this turn

# Special Actions

- Skip: Next player loses their next turn
- Draw 2: Next player must draw 2 cards from the draw pile
- Reverse: The order of play reverses 
- Wild: Current Player declares the color the wish to continue play

# Possible strategies

(1) Always play cards based on matching color and if none draw a new card
(2) always play cards based on matching number and if none draw a new card
(3) Always Play specialty cards and if none draw a new card
(4) Pick a playable card at random on every turn

# Player Order

- The player identified as player 1 will go first 
- the order will continue in a clockwise direction
- when a reverse is played the order will switch from clockwise to counterclockwise and vise versa as the game continues
- Menu will have 2 actions for player order
    SHOW PLAYER ORDER- This will display the current order of players
    ADVANCE ORDER- This will move the current player to the end of the order and advance the player that is "next" in order to be "up"

# Winning Conditions

The first player to discard all of the cards in their hand wins the game. When this happens the game will end and the rest of the players will get a score based on how many cards they have remaining in their hand. Eachcard is worth one point. The player with the lowest number of points will be in 2nd place and the order will continue with the same logic.
